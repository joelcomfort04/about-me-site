Unity Infinite Runner Mockup


Goal:

Create an infinite runner game.


Technologies Used:

C#, Unity, and Photoshop.


Obstacles:

Did not know how I was going to approch the infinite spawning of tiles or how they would generate different tiles to make it more "random".


How I Overcame Them:

Learned about how other people approached and solved this problem. I used colliders to detect when the player had moved to a tile. Then I picked from a list of different tile variations and spawned them within a random range of viable distances from the previous tile. Lots of Stack Overflow!
